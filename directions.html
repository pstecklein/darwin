Rockets

1 on 1 rockets

each hand players dealt 2 cards

higher cards win

these are examples from lowest to highest cards
  1. 5 and 7
  2. 9 and Jack
  3. pair of 2s
  4. pair of queens
  5. Aces

Aces beat any hand in the game

pairs of even the lowest value beat any mixed combination including a king and ace combo

hold if you feel your cards will beat the computer/dealer

drop if you don't have confidence in your hand

2 and 3 are the lowest possible combination, but there's an exeption.

If one player decides to hold on 23, and the other drops, the player with 23 wins regardless of the dealer hand

if a single player holds and the other drops, and the holder beats the dealer, they take the pot

If both players hold, the winner takes the pot and the loser has to pay double the current pot

If a single player holds, but loses to the dealer, they double the pot

Each player starts with 10 chips. If a payer loses all their chips, they lose. No pay no play

Basic Statistics
- YOU Darwin
Hit Points (HP) - -
Strength - -
Luck - -

Each player starts out with 200 HP, health can be affected in two ways

  1. Attacking: being attacked will negatively affect your health.
  2. Potions: Drinking a health potion will positively affect your health.

Your strength is also a very important aspect

The amount of strength in a player will determine how much damage they can deal per attack

to increase strength, drink a strength potion

Beware: a dodged attack will result in a slight decrease in strength of the attacker

Luck and Critical hits

Each round (Which consists of 1 attack each) a random luck statistic from 1 to 100 will be given to each

The higher the luck statistic the better chance the attacker gets a critical hit, which deals extra damage

Extreme low levels of luck can have side effects, for example, a very unlucky player who drinks a health potion can potentially be poisoned, resulting in a decrease in health

When you are on defense, you have 3 choices, each is a little it riskier than the next.

A counter-attack is the most risky, but it has highest reward where if you succeed, you will actually deal damage vs taking damage

A block is the safest, it will gaarentee you take some damage, but the damage will be minimized

When a player's health gets to 0, they die, game over

However, since you always attack first, Darwin gets a chance to attack from the grave if you kill him directly after your attack. So the game can only end after your defense.

Enjoy Darwin, updates including different difficulty levels and new gamemodes will be coming out soon.
